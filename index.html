At Qualivon Technologies, we specialize in developing Silicon-Photonics sensors that offer High Accuracy, Sensitivity and Specificity in terms of gas sensing.

  • Highly Sensitive Sensors down to 100s of PPB
  • Single & Multi Gas monitoring Sensors
  • Maintenance free
  • Robustness and higher longevity

Highly Sensitive Sensors down to 100s of PPB

Our sensors can identify even the smallest amount of concentration, down to hundreds of parts per billion (PPB). For crucial applications including industrial quality control, and environmental monitoring, this degree of sensitivity is necessary.

Single & Multi Gas monitoring Sensors

Multi Gas monitoring Sensors: Can detect and monitor many types of gases simultaneously.
Single Gas monitoring Sensors: Are specialized for detecting just one type of gas at higher sensitivity levels.

Maintenance free

Maintenance-free sensors eliminate the need for regular calibration, cleaning, and adjustment. They provide reliable operation, reducing expenses and downtime.

Robustness and higher longevity

Our sensor's lifetime is 5x better than conventional ones and they are robust to hazardous environments.
